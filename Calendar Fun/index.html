Calendar Fun is a calendar making company that was started by Mr. and Mrs. Fun in 2009.
The Fun family decided that calendars of the day were too bland and there was not enough
fun in them.  Life needed spicing up and this could be done, starting with the day to day use
of a calendar.  Mrs. Fun who loved photography had the idea of using cute cuddly animals as
their focus to make life more fun.  Mr. Fun took out his old printing press from his basement
and they began making cute calendars.  Today Calendar Fun is a multi-billion dollar business,
located in the Memphis Pyramid.
